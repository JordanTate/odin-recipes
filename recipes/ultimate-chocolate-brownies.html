Ultimate Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients

  • 3 Organic Eggs
  • 150g of Caster Sugar
  • 150g of Brown Sugar
  • 170g of Butter
  • 100g of Plain Flour
  • 120g of Cocoa Powder or Dark Chocolate - Or try a mixture of both!
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Bean Paste or extract (Optional)

Instructions

  • Preheat the fan oven to 180°C (356°F)
  • Melt the Butter in a small pan.
  • Add Dark Chocolate to the Butter, whisk until melted.
  • In a bowl, mix Eggs with the Vanilla Bean Paste (if used), Caster Sugar, and Brown Sugar.
  • Mix in the melted Butter and Chocolate.
  • Mix in Plain Flour with Cocoa Powder and a pinch of Salt.
  • Transfer the mix into a baking tin or tray with some baking paper, and spread it around equally.
  • Bake for 15 Minutes.
  • Allow to cool for 1 Hour after removing from the oven.
  • Enjoy!
